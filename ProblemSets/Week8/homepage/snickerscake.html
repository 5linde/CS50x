Snickers Cake

Cake

  • 10 dl cornflakes
  • 350 g unsweetened peanut butter
  • 2 dl baking syrup
  • 1 dl granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. salt

1. Place cornflakes in a large bowl and crush them into smaller pieces with your hands. Set aside.

2. Heat peanut butter, syrup, sugar, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat, stirring constantly until the sugar is melted, being careful not to burn it. Pour into the bowl with the flakes and mix well.

3. Place cornflakes in a large bowl and crush them into smaller pieces with your hands. Set aside. Heat peanut butter, syrup, sugar, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat, stirring constantly until the sugar is melted, being careful not to burn it.

4. Pour into the bowl with the flakes and mix well. Press the cornflake mixture into a baking paper-lined tray approximately 30x40 cm (for 20 pieces) in an even layer. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour until set.

Topping

  • 200 g milk chocolate
  • lake salt

1. Melt the chocolate in a bowl over a water bath. Pour over the cake and spread into an even layer.

2. Top with flake salt when the chocolate has started to set slightly.

3. Cut into pieces and eat immediately or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
