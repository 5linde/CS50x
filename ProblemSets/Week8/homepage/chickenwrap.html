Airfried Chicken Wrap

Chicken

  • 8 oz. Chicken breasts
  • Eggs
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Nature's Seasoning

1. Cut chicken breast into strips.

2. In a bowl, combine flour, cornstarch, and Nature's Seasoning.

3. Beat eggs in a separate bowl.

4. Coat each chicken strip in the flour mixture, dip in the beaten eggs, and coat again in the flour mixture.

5. Spray the air fryer basket with nonstick cooking spray.

6. Place chicken strips in the basket with space in between.

7. Cook at 380 ℉ for 8 minutes, flip, and cook for an additional 3 minutes until golden brown.

Wrap

  • Light Caesar salad dressing
  • Shredded parmesan cheese
  • Croutons
  • crunchy romaine lettuce
  • 8-inch soft tortillas
  • Cucumber, diced
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Red onion, diced

1. Chop and wash a head of romaine lettuce.

2. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce with light Caesar dressing, shredded parmesan cheese, and croutons. Toss to combine.

3. Place a ½ cup of Caesar salad on the bottom half of an 8-inch tortilla.

4. Add 3 to 4 crispy chicken strips. Fold the bottom of the tortilla over the salad.

5. Make two vertical folds on both sides of the bottom fold. Roll the tortilla upwards in a tight burrito, tucking in any loose salad.
